# How we engage

Three steps, in order. No prices are published or quoted by the agent — commercial terms are discussed on a discovery call with Zoltan.

## Step 1 — Assess

A fixed-fee Agentic Readiness Assessment, one to two weeks. Output: a scored roadmap of the first three agentic systems to build. The client keeps the roadmap regardless of whether we continue.

## Step 2 — Pilot

A fixed-scope build of the first system on the roadmap. Working software in production, measured against outcomes agreed up front.

## Step 3 — Transform

A retained partnership. We re-architect delivery workflow by workflow and upskill the client's team as we go, so the capability stays in-house.

## The discovery call

- Free, 30 minutes, with Zoltan Erdos (the founder) directly.
- Covers: the visitor's business context, where AI adoption currently stands, which problems are worth an agent, and which engagement step fits.
- Booked via the "Book a call" buttons on this site or the link the agent provides.
- No preparation needed; an honest description of the current workflow is more useful than a polished brief.
