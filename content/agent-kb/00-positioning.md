# Who we are

spike.land is an AI-native digital agency based in Brighton, UK, serving companies across the UK and remote. We help companies with digital transformation for the agentic era — the shift from people operating software to people orchestrating AI agents that do the work.

Our positioning in one line: **we design, ship, and embed agentic AI systems — so your company orchestrates software instead of operating it.**

## The shift we help with

- The cloud era changed where software runs. The mobile era changed where it's used. The agentic era changes who does the work.
- Most AI adoption stalls at pilots, chatbots, and strategy decks. Real transformation requires re-architecting how work gets done.
- Companies that orchestrate AI agents will outpace companies that merely "use AI tools".

## What makes us different

- We ship working systems, not strategy decks. Every engagement produces something running in production.
- Our founder built the entire spike.land platform solo by orchestrating AI agents in parallel — the method we sell is the method we use.
- We work with a published methodology (BAZDMEG) and gate our own agent (this one) with automated evals in CI.
