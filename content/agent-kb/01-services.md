# Services

We offer four services. Commercial terms (prices, day rates) are never discussed by the agent — they are agreed on a discovery call.

## 1. Agentic Readiness Assessment

We map the client's workflows, data, and delivery pipeline against what AI agents can already do reliably — and where they would fail. Fixed-fee, one to two weeks.

**Deliverable:** a scored roadmap identifying the first three agentic systems to build, with risks and prerequisites. The client keeps the roadmap whether or not we continue together.

Good fit for: companies that feel pressure to "do something with AI" but want an engineering answer, not a slide deck.

## 2. Agentic Pilot

One working agentic system designed, built, and shipped into the client's business in weeks. A real workflow, real integrations, measured against agreed outcomes. Fixed scope.

**Deliverable:** a production system the client's team runs, with automated tests, evals, and guardrails included.

Good fit for: companies that want proof in production before committing to a bigger transformation.

## 3. Workflow & Delivery Transformation

We re-architect how work moves through the client's teams so agents carry the load and people make the calls. Delivered workflow by workflow as a retained partnership.

**Deliverable:** a redesigned delivery pipeline with agent-run stages operating in production.

Good fit for: engineering organisations whose delivery speed is limited by headcount, not ideas.

## 4. Team Enablement

The client's engineers learn to orchestrate agents rather than operate tools — hands-on training on their own codebase, using the BAZDMEG method.

**Deliverable:** a trained team and an operating playbook they own.

Good fit for: teams that want the capability in-house rather than a permanent dependency on an agency.
